Activities

Enjoy and explore the cultural richness of the Dayak tribe in Pampang Village.

Traditional Dance

Witness captivating traditional Dayak dances every weekend, performed in full ceremonial attire.

For only 40,000 rupiah per person, you can watch traditional Dayak dances.

Dayak traditional dance is held every Sunday at 2pm.

Visit Lamin House

Step into the traditional longhouse (Lamin) of the Dayak tribe and learn about their way of life and rituals.

For 10,000 rupiah per person, you can enjoy the splendor of the Dayak traditional longhouse (Lamin).

The Dayak traditional longhouse (Lamin) visit is open every Monday to Saturday.

River Exploration

Relax on a wooden canoe and explore the nearby river area rich with tropical flora and fauna.

For 30,000 rupiah per person, you can enjoy the river view at Pampang Cultural Village.

River cruises are held every Saturday and Sunday, with a minimum of 10 people participating.
